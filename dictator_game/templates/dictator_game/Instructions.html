In this section of the experiment you are faced with the following situation:

At the beginning, Person A gets {{ endowment }}. Person B gets 0. If Person A wants, they can send money to Person B. However, Person A does not have to send any money.

Details
Person A can send any amount from 0 to 20 to B, or send 0.

Person B initially receives 0 and knows that Person A has these options. B learns A’s decision but does not make a decision and must accept A’s decision.

Payouts
For A: {{ endowment }} minus amount sent to B.
For B: amount sent by A.

Examples

  1. A sends 0 → A: {{ endowment }}, B: 0.
  2. A sends 10 → A: 10, B: 10.
  3. A sends 20 → A: 0, B: 20.
  4. A sends 4 → A: 16, B: 4.

When this section is selected for your payout, the computer will randomly pair you with another participant and randomly assign roles. Only the decision of the person in role A determines the payouts.
