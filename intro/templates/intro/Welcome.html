Welcome to the Study!

University of Oulu, Behavioural and Experimental Economics Lab &
University of Patras

Thank you for your interest in participating in our study. This research is being conducted by the University of Oulu and the University of Patras and aims to better understand decision-making and behavior in different social and economic contexts. Your participation is valuable and will contribute to advancing knowledge in the fields of behavioral and experimental economics.

What to Expect

The study consists of some economic games. It should take approximately 20 minutes to complete.

Please note:

  • All responses will be kept anonymous and confidential.
  • You may withdraw from the study at any time without providing a reason.
  • You will receive compensation in accordance with the instructions provided.

Before You Begin

Please ensure you are in a quiet environment where you can focus without interruptions. Read all instructions carefully, and feel free to take your time with each task.
