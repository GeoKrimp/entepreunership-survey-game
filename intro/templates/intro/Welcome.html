Welcome to the experiment

Thank you for taking part in this study. The experiment consists of several parts: questionnaires and decision-making tasks. In each part you will receive instructions before you make any decisions.

What you will do
  • First, you will answer a set of questionnaires about yourself.
  • Then, you will complete a series of decision-making and cognitive tasks (games).
  • In each task, please read the instructions carefully and answer as honestly as possible.
Payment

Some of the tasks are incentivised. For these tasks, your decisions or correct answers can affect your payoff.

At the end of the experiment, one of the incentivised tasks will be randomly selected for payment. Your payment from that task will be added to any fixed participation fee.

Please treat all decisions as if they were for real money.

Duration

The entire experiment is expected to take about {{ estimated_minutes }} minutes. Please make sure you have enough time and that you are in a quiet environment without distractions.

Confidentiality

All your answers will be treated confidentially and analysed only in anonymised form. There are no “right” or “wrong” personal opinions; we are interested in your genuine responses.

When you are ready to begin, click Next to proceed to the first part of the experiment.
